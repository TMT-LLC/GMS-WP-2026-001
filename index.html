The Author

Behind the Page

Grace M. Steele is a writer whose work spans the full spectrum of human experience — from the vividly imagined to the searingly real. Her writing is an act of discovery, both for herself and for the readers who follow her into the unknown.

To know her work is to know a little of who she is: curious, empathetic, and endlessly drawn to the stories that live just beneath the surface of everyday life.
